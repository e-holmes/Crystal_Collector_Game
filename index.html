Magic Gather!

Oh no! I ran out of magic! Can you help me gather some?
There are four crystals below. Each crystal will only give a certain amount of magic when you click it.
The grimoire will tell you how much magic you need! Don't gather too much or the spell won't work!
When you get the right amount of magic you can move on to another spell by click the broomstick!
The crystals will give a different amount of magic for each spell!
